Why Children?

Children are the foundation of the future of our country.
So that kids might grow up to be tomorrow's scientists, legislators, athletes,
journalists, and teachers, let's invest in their potential today!


Children make up 472 million of India's population, or 40% of the total.

Nevertheless, the Union Budget for 2021-2022 only set aside 2.46% for their welfare and development.
Actually, the National Plan of Action for Children suggests that the nation's budgetary allotment for children should account for 5% of GDP; however,
for the past two years, it has remained steady at 0.43% of GDP.
