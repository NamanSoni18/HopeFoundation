Children make up 472 million of India's population, or 40% of the total.

Nevertheless, the Union Budget for 2021-2022 only set aside 2.46% for their welfare and development.
Actually, the National Plan of Action for Children suggests that the nation's budgetary allotment for children should account for 5% of GDP; however,
for the past two years, it has remained steady at 0.43% of GDP.

The Importance Of Children's Rights

According to the United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child (UNCRC), which was ratified by India in 1992,
all children have fundamental rights which must be recognized by governments and citizens alike. At HopeFoundation,
we're committed to doing everything we can to ensure the rights of India's children and so, work on 4 key programmatic areas.

Education Right To Development

A quality education equips individuals with the knowledge, skills, and critical thinking necessary to participate meaningfully in society. It empowers them to make informed decisions, improve their livelihoods, and advocate for themselves and others. This ripple effect extends beyond individuals, driving economic growth, reducing poverty and inequality, and fostering democratic values.

Education is essential for addressing global challenges like climate change, health pandemics, and conflict. It equips people with the tools to understand these issues, find solutions, and build more resilient communities.

Hope Foundation Programs Focus on:

Early Childhood Education

0-3 years

The early years are a magical time when the brain forms its most critical connections, influencing everything from learning and memory to resilience and emotional intelligence. By providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment filled with play-based learning, quality ECE programs equip children with the essential skills they need to thrive. From literacy and numeracy to problem-solving and social-emotional intelligence, ECE lays the groundwork for success in school and beyond.

School Readiness

3-6 years

A strong foundation in early childhood sets the stage for lifelong learning and success. School readiness programs bridge the gap between the nurturing environment of early childhood education and the structured world of formal school.

Learning Outcomes

6-18 years

Children's learning outcomes are influenced by their continuous access to high-quality education. Ensuring that children are enrolled in school and do not drop out before reaching the appropriate educational age is thus a critical area of intervention.

Health & Nutrition

proper nutrition is crucial for brain development, laying the groundwork for cognitive skills, resilience, and lifelong learning. A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins fuels young bodies and minds, setting them up for success in school and beyond.

Proper nutrition isn't just about filling bellies; it's about building brains. In those critical early years, the human brain undergoes a whirlwind of development, forming delicate neural pathways that will influence everything from memory and concentration to learning and social skills. These intricate connections are fueled by a symphony of nutrients, each playing a vital role in the orchestra of cognition. Think of fruits and vegetables as the colorful conductors, bursting with vitamins and antioxidants that orchestrate healthy neural growth. Whole grains act as the sturdy base notes, providing steady energy to keep young minds focused and ready to learn. Lean proteins play the melodic leads, building strong nerve fibers that carry information efficiently. And don't forget the essential supporting cast - healthy fats, the smooth lubricants that ensure seamless communication between brain cells.

Hope Foundation Right to Survival Focus on:

Care for Expectant and Newborns

The Hope Foundation is dedicated to ensuring the well-being and health of expectant mothers and newborns through our comprehensive program, "Care for Expectant and Newborns." Our initiative recognizes the critical importance of maternal and child health, aiming to provide support, education, and resources to enhance the overall experience of pregnancy and infancy for vulnerable communities.

Growth Monitoring

Growth monitoring for children is a pivotal aspect of the Hope Foundation's mission to ensure the well-being and development of every child under its care. This essential initiative involves systematically tracking and assessing a child's physical, mental, and emotional growth over time, providing crucial insights into their overall health and nutritional status. By implementing a comprehensive growth monitoring program, the Hope Foundation aims to address and alleviate the challenges that children face in their formative years, contributing to their long-term health and prosperity.

Learning Outcomes

Learning outcomes for children are critical aspects that contribute significantly to their holistic development. At Hope Foundation, our commitment to empowering children through education revolves around fostering a range of learning outcomes that extend beyond academic achievements. Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals who not only excel academically but also possess the skills, values, and resilience needed to navigate life's challenges.

Safety & Protection

The Hope Foundation, dedicated to the welfare of children, places safety and protection at the forefront of its mission. Recognizing the vulnerability of children and the need for a nurturing environment, our NGO has implemented comprehensive initiatives to ensure the well-being of every child under our care. Safety begins with creating a physical environment that is secure and free from potential hazards. At the heart of our efforts is the establishment of safe spaces where children can learn, play, and grow without fear. We prioritize the maintenance and regular inspection of facilities to guarantee that they adhere to stringent safety standards. This includes ensuring structural integrity, implementing fire safety measures, and providing appropriate medical resources.

Hope Foundation Right To Protection Focus on:

Child Labour

Child labor is a deeply distressing global issue that continues to afflict countless children around the world, undermining their fundamental rights, health, and future prospects. The Hope Foundation, recognizing the gravity of this problem, is committed to raising awareness and actively combating child labor to secure a brighter and more dignified future for these vulnerable individuals.

Child Marriage

Child marriage is a deeply entrenched and pervasive issue affecting millions of children globally, and it stands as a violation of their fundamental rights. At the heart of this social malaise lies the grim reality that children, particularly young girls, are forced into marriage before they attain the legal age, often robbing them of their childhood and subjecting them to a life of hardship and adversity.

Child Traffickings

Child trafficking is a grave and pervasive issue that poses a severe threat to the well-being and future of countless children around the world. For organizations like the Hope Foundation, combating this heinous crime is not just a mission but a moral imperative. Child trafficking involves the recruitment, transportation, transfer, harboring, or receipt of children through force, coercion, or deception for various exploitative purposes, including forced labor, sexual exploitation, and involvement in armed conflicts.
